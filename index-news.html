About EagleGIS
EagleGIS is a municipal meeting document tracker built for the Village of Estero, Florida. It provides searchable access to Village Council and Planning, Zoning & Design Board meeting minutes from 2015–2026, organized by meeting type: Regular Council Meeting, Council Workshop, Special Called, Joint, Public Hearing, Quasi-judicial Hearing, and the PZDB sub-types.

Each record retains its location and lat/long for ArcGIS integration.

The Community News tab pulls live articles from EsteroToday.com, our community partner's news site covering Estero growth, transportation, safety, environment, and development.

Built by FGCU Team 4 as part of COP 3710 Database Systems, Spring 2026. Community partner: Engage Estero.
