About EagleGIS
EagleGIS is a municipal meeting document tracker built for the Village of Estero, Florida. It provides searchable access to Village Council and Planning Board meeting minutes from 2015–2026, covering infrastructure projects including the BERT Rail Trail, Septic to Sewer conversions, and Corkscrew Road improvements.

Built by FGCU Team 4 as part of COP 3710 Database Systems, Spring 2026. Community partner: Engage Estero.
